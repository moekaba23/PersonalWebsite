URBAN HEALTH PLAN

Case Study

Urban Health Plan is a system of community health centers in New York City. As a patient myself that attends one of their clinics, I find their web portal for patients to be very outdated and difficult to use. Hence came the idea to redesign it.

The Problem:

  • Outdated and clunky interface.
  • Poor layout.
  • Inconsistent design choices.
  • Bad typography.

Current Web Portal

The Solutions:

  • Decrease menu options(declutter).
  • Modern and clean design.
  • Good layout and better use of space.
  • Improved typography.

Site Map

User Interviews

I conducted in person interviews with 6 patients at two different clinic branches. The user interviews helped us to get an idea of what our users were looking for and to gather as much information from them as possible before moving forward.
