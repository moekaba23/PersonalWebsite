NBC Elections Web Products

The goal of this project was to redesign NBCUniversal's best in class internal web products that drive award winning Elections Coverage. I worked with the Elections Data Team consisting of front-end & back-end engineers in a SCRUM environment.

My Role

UI/UX Designer

Interaction Designer

Duration

6 months

Tools

Figma

Storybook

Execution

1. Redesigned internal web dashboard and kept consistent theme across all NBC Elections products.

2. Included data visualization with the aim of reducing the strain of going through large amounts of data for our users.

3. Created design system and integrated reusable components into Storybook for our front-end engineers.

4. Created high fidelity prototypes for demos and usability testing on our target users.
