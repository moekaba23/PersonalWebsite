Case Study

The Problem

Many investors are forced to have their stocks, retirement accounts and cryptocurrencies on separate platforms. Although there are valid reasons to why that is, such as compliance issues, it is still a major inconvenience to have to open different apps to view and manage your different portfolios.

The Solution

We decided to come up with a platform that allows investors to buy, sell and check on their stocks, retirement accounts and cryptocurrencies in one place.

Design Process

User Interviews

Competition Analysis

I enjoyed conducting user research by doing user interviews and converting the initial concept to designs based on feedback received.
